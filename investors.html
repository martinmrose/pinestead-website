Information for Investors

Pinestead Succession is undergoing a search process to identify an exceptional UK business to acquire. This process is led by our succession entrepreneur, who will also take over the day-to-day management of the acquired business.

We have secured capital for the search process and may selectively bring on additional capital from value-adding partners. The existing investor base has no single dominant investor and comprises family offices, private investors and funds highly experienced in small-company buyouts.
