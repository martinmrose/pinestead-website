What we offer

We're searching for a high-quality UK business to acquire, manage and grow for the long term. Here's what you can expect:

  • Continued independence of your business, brand and legacy — we don't absorb the company into a larger entity.
  • Dedication. We are not running a portfolio of businesses; our entrepreneur takes over as the new CEO/MD from day one.
  • Decisions made for the long term — good for your business, your employees and your customers.
  • A significant upfront payout with clear, agreed terms.
  • A smooth transition, guided by business owners who have done it before.

We are a team of business operators and investors from the UK and Europe, led by one "succession entrepreneur" who will personally take over the day-to-day operations of your business.
